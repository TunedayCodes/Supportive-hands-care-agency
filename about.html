About Us

At Supportive Hands Care Agency, we are dedicated to providing top-quality care services for the elderly, reliable nanny care, and exceptional housekeeping. Our mission is to make life easier for families by offering trusted and compassionate services.

Our team of experienced caregivers, nannies, and housekeepers are committed to providing personalized care tailored to your loved one's needs. We take the time to get to know each of our clients and their families, so we can create a care plan that meets their unique needs and preferences.

Our team are more than just staff, we are trained empathetic partners who strive to make a meaningful difference everyday. We use innovative practices and personal insights to create a warm, safe environment where your loved ones can thrive.


Our Story

Our story began with a simple but profound mission: to bring aid, warmth, compassion and excellence to those who need it most, to transform traditional caregiving into a personalized experience that honors the unique needs and expectation of every individual we serve.

We're founded bt a team with a deep-rooted experience of caregiving and giving helping hands to those who need it most, dedicated health care professionals with great passion for delivering exceptional care, because we understand the impact that empathetic support can have on people's lives. we believe that life's greatest joys are found in the simplest moments of care, support, connection and comfort.

Due to an increase in number of working adults, there is a rising need for care-givers for elderly and children, also an increasing need for housekeeping services. The team is also set on the opportunity to establish an agency that would truly put people first, tailoring our services to meet individual's unique needs.
