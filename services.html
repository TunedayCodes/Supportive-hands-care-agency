Our Services

At Supportive Hands Care Agency, we offer a range of services designed to support individuals and families in need. Our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client, and we strive to provide exceptional care and support in every way.



Elderly Care

Our elderly care services are designed to support seniors in their daily lives, providing assistance with activities such as bathing, dressing, and medication management.


  • Personal care and hygiene
  • Medication management
  • Meal preparation and nutrition
  • Light housekeeping and laundry


Nanny Care

Our nanny care services provide trusted and experienced caregivers to support families with childcare needs.


  • Childcare and education
  • Meal preparation and snack time
  • Homework assistance and tutoring


Housekeeping

Our housekeeping services provide trusted and experienced housekeepers that takes care of your home and give it that spankly touch of beauty.


  • Cleaning Rooms and Public Areas: Dusting, sweeping, vacuuming, mopping floors, and cleaning windows, mirrors, and furnitures.
  • Making Beds: Changing and arranging linens, pillowcases, and bed covers.
  • Laundry Duties: Washing, drying, ironing, folding, and replacing towels, sheets, and clothing items (if required).
  • Restocking Supplies: Ensuring guest amenities such as toiletries, towels, toilet paper, and other items are replenished.
  • Waste Management/Sanitizing: Emptying trash bins and disposing of waste appropriately. Disinfecting high-touch areas such as door handles, remotes, and bathroom surfaces to maintain hygiene.
